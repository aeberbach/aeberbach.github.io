Why "Gray Unicorn"?

Domain names are hard to come by and this one wasn’t taken. A gray unicorn is a very useful creature in Nethack. If you're not playing a neutral character it is safe to sacrifice, you can always throw gems at it for luck, its horn cures poison too. If you don’t know what that’s about you should try Nethack, the best game ever.
